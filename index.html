Welcome to Care Forward Project

Care Forward Project allows anyone to donate or purchase care package items for friends, family, or even strangers. Each box is thoughtfully curated for themes like mental health, self-care, hobbies, and aesthetics. You can buy items directly for a box, donate to help fund boxes, or write personal notes to be included.
